Tang Pie

Description

This tang pie is perfect for summer gatherings and only takes minutes to put together.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup orange-flavored drink mix (e.g. Tang)
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 prepared graham cracker crust
  • 1 container frozen whipped topping, thawed, divided
  • 8 mandarin orange segments for garnish

Steps

  1. Combine orange drink mix, condensed milk, and cream cheese in a large bowl. Beat with an electric mixer on high speed until well combined. Fold 2 cups whipped topping into cream cheese mixture.
  2. Pour mixture into graham cracker crust and chill until firm, at least 1 hour. Garnish with remaining whipped topping and mandarin orange segments.
